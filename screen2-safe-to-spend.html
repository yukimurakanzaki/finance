What changed from v1.0

•Rp 577k ÷ weeks, a fixed stipend that ignored commitments and spending.
•Now derived: pool − subs − weekend, then ÷ workdays left.
•Recalculates mid-week — overspend Monday and Tuesday's ceiling drops (fuel-range).
•Weekend carved first, so the workweek number is honest.
•Savings (pipe + DPLK) shown at the top, gone before discretionary — not at the bottom as leftover.
